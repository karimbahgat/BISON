Built by the William & Mary geoLab, the Boundary Lookup is an online tool and database for geocoding textual name references to administrative boundaries (i.e., state, county) to their spatial representation in any country in the world. We currently search administrative unit names against a database of approximately 1.3 million boundaries from all areas of the world.
